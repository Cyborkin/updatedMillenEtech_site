Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Recipe Management

Save, organize, and search all your recipes in one place. Import from any source or build your own from scratch. Every ingredient tracked automatically.

Weekly Meal Scheduling

Plan breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks across the whole week in a clean drag-and-drop calendar. See your nutritional spread at a glance.

Smart Grocery Lists

Mallo consolidates ingredients from your entire week into a single, de-duplicated shopping list — sorted by store section. No more buying three things you already have.

How It Works

Three steps.
Real results.

01

Add your recipes

Drop in a URL, type one in manually, or browse Mallo's starter library. Every ingredient gets parsed and stored cleanly.

02

Plan your week

Drag meals into your weekly calendar. Mallo shows you your nutritional balance and flags gaps in your plan.

03

Shop smarter

Tap "Generate List" and get a consolidated, sorted grocery list ready to go. Check items off as you shop.
