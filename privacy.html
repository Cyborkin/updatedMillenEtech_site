Privacy Policy

We believe privacy is a right, not a setting. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how MillenETech ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with Mallo and our other products and services. By using our apps or services, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and basic profile details (such as a display name). This is the minimum needed to identify your account and send you important service updates.

Content you create

We store content you input into the app — including recipes, meal plans, and grocery lists. This data lives in your account and is used solely to provide the app's core functionality.

Diagnostic data

We collect app performance data and crash logs to identify and fix technical issues. This data is anonymized and never tied back to individual identifiable records.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core app functionality — meal planning, recipe storage, and grocery list generation
  • Maintain the security and reliability of our services
  • Troubleshoot technical problems and improve app performance
  • Send you account-related communications (never marketing without your consent)

We do not use your data to train external AI models or for advertising purposes.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Period.

We may share data with a limited number of trusted service providers who help us operate the app (for example, cloud hosting or authentication services). These providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and may only use your data to provide services to us — never for their own purposes.

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the app. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

5. Your Choices

Edit or delete content

You can modify or remove any recipe, meal plan, or grocery list at any time directly within the app.

Delete your account

Email support@millenetech.com with the subject line "Delete my account" from the email address associated with your account. We'll confirm deletion within 30 days.

Access your data

You may request a copy of the data we hold about you by contacting us at the email above.

6. Children

Mallo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

7. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or how we handle your data? We want to hear from you.
